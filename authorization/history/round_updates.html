Round 2 Updates

Mobsters will no longer be arrested when traveling in-between cities.

Families color's will now be displayed next to the family box on a player's profile page.

Added Local Mobsters page to display locally based mobsters and their networth.

When a player is killed they will no longer be able to revive themselves via the hospital. They will need to re-create an entirely new character and start over from scratch.

The store has been removed to allow items to be achieved from various jobs.

The payout's for all group jobs including family jobs have been balanced out.

Capo's can now purchase casinos.

Fixed an issue with the "My Prostitutes" table being out of order.

Removed "Invite Friends" from facebook.

You'll need a lockpick in order to break into houses and to steal a vehicle.

Added an android app for the game which can be found via the google play store.

There are now several different types of organizations you can start: Crime Family, Irish Mob, Russian Bratva, Street Gang, Motorcycle Club, Yakuza & Triads.

Changed Vote For Us Link on the menu to Free Energy.

When you have less then 500 energy a link will appear at the bottom of the game displaying options for free energy.
